Featured Project

Sniffr

Sniffr was an app design that I completed during my User Experience/User interface design class. Sniffr focuses on finding dogs available for adoption that suit each individuals needs, and helps shelters find owners suitable for the needs of each dog. This is achieved with an advanced filtering system designed to show user's the profiles of dogs that most accurately fit their lifestyle.

As an animal lover, the concept and design of Sniffr is one that I hope to bring into existence in the form of a mobile and/or web application.
